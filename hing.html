What is Hing (Asafoetida)?

Hing, also known as Asafoetida, is a spice derived from the resin of the Ferula plant, mostly found in the Middle East and Central Asia. Its strong, pungent aroma transforms when cooked, adding a deep umami and savory flavor.

For centuries, it has been a cornerstone of Indian cooking, Ayurvedic medicine, and digestive wellness.
