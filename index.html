What is Hing (Asafoetida)?

Hing, also known as Asafoetida, is a spice derived from the resin of the ferula plant, mostly found in the Middle East and Central Asia. Its strong pungent aroma transforms when cooked, giving a deep umami and savory flavor. For centuries, it has been a cornerstone of Indian cooking, Ayurvedic medicine, and digestive wellness.
